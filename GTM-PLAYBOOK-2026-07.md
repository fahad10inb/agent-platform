# Real-estate operator — go-to-market playbook (2026-07-08)

The product is built and verified live (5 modules). The need now is ONE paying customer, not
more features. This is the plan to get there. Honest premise: as a solo founder with no logo
yet, you don't cold-close Emaar. You climb: warm pilot → reference logos → up-market.

## The blocker to clear first (this week)
- **WhatsApp outbound delivery** needs a Meta approved template (reminders, nurture, instant
  outreach). Until then: **a pilot still runs fully on the web widget + INBOUND WhatsApp** (when
  a customer messages first, you're in the free 24h window — no template needed). So you can
  START pilots now on inbound; wire the template in parallel to unlock outbound.
- Also do the 10-min ops items: RESEND_API_KEY (owner lead emails), ENVIRONMENT=production, a
  domain. These make demos look real.

## Target ladder (who to approach, in order of reachability × value)
1. **Your warm network — do this FIRST.** Any agent/agency owner you know personally, or one
   intro away. The goal is a free pilot + a testimonial + learning what they'd pay for. One warm
   yes beats fifty cold emails.
2. **Small/mid independent brokerages (5–30 agents).** Reachable, fast decisions, owner IS the
   buyer. These are your reference logos. Find them: Bayut/Property Finder agency pages (contact
   info is public), Instagram (active RE agencies), LinkedIn (principal brokers).
3. **Off-plan master-agencies / brokers who sell developer stock.** More reachable than a
   developer's HQ, and they feel the launch-day lead surge directly. This is the bridge to the
   whale.
4. **Developers (Emaar, DAMAC, Sobha, Binghatti, Nakheel…) — later, with references.** Approach
   their sales/marketing directors via LinkedIn or a warm intro AFTER you have 2–3 brokerage
   logos and a real ROI number to show.

## How to reach them
- **LinkedIn** — search "principal broker" / "sales director" / "head of off-plan" + Dubai;
  short personalized message (see pitch below). Best channel for the up-market targets.
- **Public agency contacts** on Bayut / Property Finder pages — WhatsApp/call the owner directly.
- **Instagram DMs** to active RE agencies (they live there).
- **Events** — Cityscape Global (Dubai), property expos, broker meetups. Face-to-face + a live
  demo closes far better than any message.
- **Warm intros** — ask everyone you know for one intro to a broker/agency owner.

## The pitch (money-first, 30 seconds)
Never pitch "AI" or features. Pitch the leak:
> "When a buyer messages your agents at 11pm or during a viewing, who answers? The first agency
> to reply wins the deal ~78% of the time [cite as industry research]. I've built an AI that
> answers every lead in seconds, in Arabic and English, on WhatsApp — qualifies them, matches
> your listings, books the viewing, and hands you a scored lead. It respects Dubai's advertising
> rules — it won't quote an unpermitted listing or invent a price. You switch it on today, no new
> CRM, no migration. Let me put it live on your real leads free for two weeks and you decide."

For a DEVELOPER/master-agency, sharpen it to the launch surge:
> "On launch day you get thousands of leads in hours and your team can touch maybe 15% in the
> first hour — the rest go cold. This qualifies 100% of them instantly, in Arabic, and routes the
> hot ones to your closers inside the 5-minute window. Each rescued lead is a 4–8% commission on
> a 7-figure unit. It pays for itself on one deal."

## The demo (this is what actually closes)
Don't send a deck — do it live:
1. Import THEIR listings (CSV/Sheet/XML) in front of them in 2 minutes.
2. Hand them the widget; let them chat as a buyer — watch it match their real properties, qualify,
   and score the lead.
3. Push it on an unpermitted listing → it refuses to quote (the compliance moment that reassures
   a compliance-paranoid owner).
4. Show the owner dashboard: the scored lead sitting where they'd look.
The demo IS the pitch. When they see it work on their own business, the objection dissolves.

## The offer (remove all risk)
- **Free 2-week pilot on their real leads. You set it up. No migration, no contract.** If it's not
  catching them business, they walk away, no cost.
- Then price: **founding AED 500–750/mo** for small shops to get logos; **AED 30k–100k/yr** for a
  large brokerage; **AED 60k–200k/yr or per-launch** for a developer. Anchor against one missed
  deal a year and the AED 45–60k/yr they already pay the portals.

## Objection handling (the four you'll hear)
- "I have a receptionist" → it catches what she can't: nights, Fridays, three calls at once.
- "Customers hate bots" → it says it's an AI, handles the simple stuff, hands to a human instantly.
- "It'll say something wrong" → it only answers from what YOU give it, never invents a price, and
  you see every conversation. (Show the permit gate.)
- "Too expensive" → free pilot, and it pays for itself on one saved deal.

## First-week action checklist
- [ ] Wire WhatsApp (Meta app + one approved utility template) → unlocks outbound.
- [ ] Set RESEND_API_KEY + ENVIRONMENT=production on Render; buy a domain.
- [ ] List 10 warm/reachable targets (people you know + 5 independent brokerages from the portals).
- [ ] Send 5 personalized LinkedIn/WhatsApp messages using the pitch above.
- [ ] Book ONE live demo. Import their listings, run the buyer flow, show the scored lead.
- [ ] Land ONE free pilot. Learn what they'd pay 5 figures for → THAT is the next thing to build.

## The one rule
Sell BEFORE you build more. The next advancement (Developer/off-plan mode, voice, dashboards)
should be the thing your first real buyer tells you they'd pay for — not a guess. One pilot
teaches you more than another month of code.

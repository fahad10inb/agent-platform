# First-contact kit — validate the pain AND start the sale (2026-07-08)

Copy-paste ready. Goal of every conversation: get them to name the pain in their own words
(validation) and book a 15-min live demo (the sale). Talk to 5 this week.

## Step 1 — the outreach message (LinkedIn / WhatsApp / Instagram DM)
Short, personal, no pitch, just a question. Send 5.

> "Hi [name] — I build tools for Dubai real-estate agencies and I'm trying to understand one
> thing properly before I get it wrong: how agencies handle the leads that come in after hours or
> when everyone's in viewings. Could I ask you 3 quick questions — 5 minutes, no pitch? Genuinely
> just want your take as someone who lives it."

Why it works: you're asking for their expertise, not selling. Owners answer that. If they reply,
you're in a conversation — the sale starts itself.

## Step 2 — the 3 questions (validation + wedge, in order)
Ask conversationally, listen more than you talk. Each does a job:

**Q1 — surface the pain:**
> "When a lead comes in from Bayut or Property Finder — say at 9pm, or when your agents are all in
> viewings — how fast does someone actually reply?"
- Listen for: "not fast," "sometimes hours," "we try but…". That's your wedge confirmed.
- If they say "instantly, we're great at it" — ask "even at 2am on a Friday?" The gap appears.

**Q2 — quantify it in THEIR words (this becomes your ROI number):**
> "Out of all the leads you pay the portals for, roughly what share do you think never really get
> a proper follow-up?"
- Whatever number they say — 20%, 40%, "honestly no idea" — is gold. Let THEM say it. Now the
  leak has a size, in their own mouth: "so you're paying [45–60k/yr] and losing [X%] of it."

**Q3 — open the door to the solution + demo:**
> "If something could answer and qualify every one of those leads instantly — in Arabic and
> English, on WhatsApp, and hand your agent a scored lead — would that be worth a look?"
- ~Always yes. Then: "Give me 15 minutes — I'll load YOUR listings and show you it working on a
  real buyer conversation. If it's not obviously useful, I'll get out of your way."

## Step 3 — the transition to the demo (don't oversell, just book it)
> "Perfect. Send me your website or a few of your listings and I'll have it ready. When's good —
> [tomorrow] or [day after]?"
Then run the live demo from the GTM playbook: import their listings, let them chat as a buyer,
show the scored lead + the permit refusal.

## What you're really doing
- 5 messages → 2–3 conversations → the pain validated (or a NEW pain surfaced worth building) →
  1–2 demos booked → 1 free pilot.
- If all 5 name "we lose leads / slow response" → you own the wedge; go hard on it.
- If they name something you DON'T solve → that's your next build, now validated by a real buyer.

## The mindset
You are not begging for a sale — you're a builder asking experts about their world, and the
solution happens to be in your pocket. That framing gets replies, gets truth, and closes.
